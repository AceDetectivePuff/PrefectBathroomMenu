Caitatoes' Dragon Egg Quiche with Summer Vegetables! The dragon guards her hoard jealously. Also, had to make a Gryffindor Inspired drink!

Thinly slice the dragon's garden. Half a zucchini and half a yellow squash, one bell pepper, 5-6 mushrooms, half an onion, one small potato, 5 cloves garlic.

Fry with a few tablespoons butter (the more butter the more flavor, of course.) Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, dill, rosemary. The dragon will practically thank you for taking its vegetables!

So the fried veg doesn't cook the eggs when they hit, let it cool off some and add about 1/4 cup milk to the pan for faster cooling. Mix with 8 dragon eggs.

Pour into your tin - the dragon once again must watch over its gold (get it? it's yolks). Bake at 350F for 40 minutes.

The finished product, straight out the oven!

The dragon seems pleased with the results. It can add this quiche to its hoard (and also eat it for breakfast, like I will.)
