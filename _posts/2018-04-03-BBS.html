Take a pasteurised egg and knife. Start cutting.

Egg cut in half. Separate the yolk and whites. Save the whites in the fridge.

Cure the egg yolk.

Gently cover with salt.

Bury it!

Voila! After 2 days, the egg yolk is cured, hardened and ... ... ... rubbery.

Rinse and pat dry.

Shape to a duck shape! Add an eye.

Whisk up the whites.

Pour whites in the egg shell "bathtub" and serve with the "rubber duck".

Warning. There is no heating process. Please do not eat raw eggs!.
