For this recipe you will need:

1 cup unsalted butter softened
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 cups all purpose flour
2/3 cup coarse sparkling sugar
1/2 cup crushed hard candies Jolly Ranchers or Lifesavers

In the bowl of a mixer, beat the butter and sugar until smooth, about 2 minutes.
Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.

Add salt, then slowly mix in the flour until thoroughly combined.

It should become smooth and shinny, also less sticky.

Wrap the plastic wrap around the dough and chill for at least 1 hour, or up to 1 day before you plan to bake your cookies.

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper or a nonstick baking mat

Put the hard candy into plastic bags then smash them!

Cut your cookies and let them bake for a few minutes.

Remove the cookies from the oven and add the candy.

Bake for 10-12 minutes or until cookies are light golden brown and the candy has melted. Cool completely.
