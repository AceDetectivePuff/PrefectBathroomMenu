Ask Proffesor Sprout for two yellow Badger potatoes!

Fillet and wash mermaid meat (or whitefish and perch in leaner times)

Cut potatoes with severing charm into half inch thick fries

wash and dry fries

dry fish

heat high temprature oil in cast iron dutch oven. (may require help from teacher if your incendio spell isn't up to par)

add fries into oil in batches

fry until golden

salt fries

coat mermaid lighty with flour

mix dry batter with up to a full can of butter beer!

dredge mermaid coated with flour in batter mix

add immidietly to oil

cook until gold

transfer to rack to drain off oil

2/3 of the way there!

boil peas

add peas to blender (a nifty muggle invention!)

add butter

add pepper

add salt

add heavy cream

blend lightly. it should be chunky!

serve to friends and enjoy!
